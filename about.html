About us


Overview

Agro fruits is committed to deliver the high-quality Egyptian fruits and vegetables to worldwide markets with the desired criteria and market preferences for each customer and according to the different governmental legistations and protocols.

So, we are up to dates with the market requirements and dynamics, plus controlling the documentary processes professionally. We take care of our products starting from the farm till reach our customers in the best conditions as if they are just picked and in line with the international standards.

Our Vision

Our main aim is to be one of the premier global exporters of fresh fruits and vegetables that through presenting premium quality by recently techniques to achieve exceptional services with competitive price with our competitors in the market.

Our Mission

To ensure highly quality for all our products, firstly We control the farming operations from seeding to harvesting then sorting , the crops are sorted to separate the seen defects initially then Washing is a mandatory processing step in fruits and vegetables processing line, in order to retain the freshness and pureness of the products then washing to avoid any damage for fruits finally we package our products well with recently technique .

We provide a distinguished service through an integrated system with the highest efficiency team, specialized quality engineers, and experts in sea and air shipping.

With a team of highly competent experts, agro fruits company is in perfect control of the evolutions of the world market. It offers export services, trading (to facilitate the procedures and facilitate the completion of the financial transaction), as well as advice and supervision.

Our Packing House

Our Facility uses an electronic sizer (Globalscan 6) which allows the selection of the fruits by their weight, diameter, color, shape, and external quality of the skin, for a production capacity of 70 t/h.

The packing house also includes a large refrigerated storage area for fruit, which remains better conserved.

By 2026 we will develop our cool rooms to contain 2000 tones, plus 7 pre-cooling rooms to optimize special protocols requirements and finalize the system traceability setup for our products.
