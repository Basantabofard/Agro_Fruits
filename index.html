About us

Agro fruits international co, one of premier global exporter of agriculture Egyptian production of high quality, our experience in the field extends more than ten years. the company is located in the Delta, where is a major center for agricultural lands within Egypt that enables us to constantly ensure the soil and products’ quality compliance with global food safety standards throughout all stages, from farming till our packing house. We have the ability to supply a lot of varieties as our customers’ requirements, we specialized in the export of all kinds of fresh fruits and vegetables, our company exports have reached a wide range of international markets (Europe \ Asia \ Africa) with large quantities. Argo fruits have dynamic team and highly qualified managers employ strict quality control standard through our growing, harvesting till the packing of our products, we do continue progress in our system and strategies to present the finest fruits and vegetables with distinguished services that suitable with our customers’ requirements. We use highly technology and increasing innovation to introduce a unique product to satisfy the ever-changing market trends in the world.
